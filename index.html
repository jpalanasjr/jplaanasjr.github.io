Food Security

Food security in the Philippines refers to the state in which all individuals have physical and economic access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food to meet their dietary needs and food preferences for an active and healthy life. It involves ensuring that food is available, accessible, and affordable for the entire population at all times. In the Philippines, we are familiar of the term "Isang kayod, isang tuka" it is a Filipino proverb that roughly translates to "One toils, one eats" in English. This phrase conveys the idea that one must work hard or exert effort in order to sustain oneself or achieve success. It emphasizes the importance of hard work, perseverance, and the correlation between effort and reward.
